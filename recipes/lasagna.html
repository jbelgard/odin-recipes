Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a great dish that is easy to prepare and the whole family will love.

It requires baking in the oven so don't forget the pan.

World Famous Lasagna can be made in your kitchen tonight.

    Ingredients
  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg
    Steps
  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
